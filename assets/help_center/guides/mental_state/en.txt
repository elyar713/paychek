Mental State: Your Trader "Bio-Data"
Performance is not only a matter of technique; it is a matter of human balance. The Mental State page is your biological dashboard: it analyzes how your daily habits directly impact your financial results.

[img:assets/help_center/mental_state_impact_settings.gif]

1. Daily Psychology Operation
Each day, perform your "psycho check-up." Use the sliders to rate your performance pillars:

Customizable routine: Sleep, exercise, meditation, or any other element you consider vital for your personality.

Calibrated impact: Each slider has an impact setting (positive or negative). You define yourself what helps you stay "sharp" in the markets.

Performance ring: Instantly visualize your overall state for the day via a ring chart that aggregates all your personal data.

2. Expert Configuration (The Gear)
Click the gear icon in the section to define the weight (as a percentage) of each factor in your overall state. If for you sleep accounts for 60% of your success and meditation for 10%, PAYCHEK lets you configure that reality.

3. Performance & Psychology Correlation
This is where PAYCHEK becomes an exceptional diagnostic tool. Your Mental State data is synchronized in real time with your trades:

[img:assets/help_center/mental_state_calendar.gif]

Visual history: Use the calendar to navigate into the past and see how your mental state evolved.

Automatic correlation analysis: The Performance page now displays cause-and-effect links:

Example: "When your mental state is at 50%, your Winrate drops to 44%." This visibility lets you know exactly which day you should close your trading platform to protect your capital.

4. Customizing Your Sessions
You are not limited to a standard trading day. PAYCHEK adapts to your life and your market hours.

Session configuration (The Gear): Click the gear icon in the calendar toolbar to define your own trading time slots.

Total flexibility: Whether you trade during the London, New York session, or have offset hours, you are free to choose the hours that make up your "trading day."
