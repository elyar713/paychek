📊 Performance : Your Trading Scanner
The Performance page is Paychek's analytical hub. It does more than tally profits—it cross‑references your technical, psychological, and strategic data to surface strengths and fix structural weaknesses.

[img:assets/help_center/performance_overview.png]

1. Paychek Lens — Your discipline score 🔍
The Paychek Lens summarizes your overall trader health.

Psychological score: Average mental state when you open positions.

Rules respect: How closely you stick to your "Golden Rules".

Strategy efficiency: Quality of your pre‑trade planning.

Tip: Negative P&L with a strong Paychek Lens means you’re on track. The opposite suggests you're gambling rather than executing a plan.

[img:assets/help_center/performance_discipline_impact.png]

2. Alerts & warnings (discipline check) ⚠️
Paychek watches your limits in real time based on My Strategy settings:

Max loss exceeded: Shown when you breach your daily loss cap.

Out of session: Flags trades outside your optimal trading windows.

Overtrading: Warns when daily/weekly trade counts exceed what you configured.

[img:assets/help_center/performance_temporal_cards.png]

3. Time & duration insights ⏳
Improve timing instead of trading in a vacuum:

Performance by hour: Spot your best windows and avoid hours where win rate sinks.

Position duration: Find your holding "sweet spot"—15 minutes versus four hours—with average outcomes by bucket.

[img:assets/help_center/performance_duration_volume.png]

4. Strategy & setup reporting 📈
Stop guessing what works:

Win rate by setup: Compare setups (for example SMC vs chart patterns).

Discipline drill‑downs: See where you break rules most often.
