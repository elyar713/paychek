Add a Trade: Precision in Service of Analysis
Adding a trade in PAYCHEK is designed to be as fast as possible while capturing the crucial data for your progress.

[img:assets/help_center/add_trade.gif]

Intuitive entry: Select your asset and market in just a few clicks. Use our quick-fill shortcuts to avoid repetitive manual entry.

Automatic calculation: Simply enter your entry and exit prices. PAYCHEK instantly calculates the result of your trade, including commissions that you can configure via the gear icon next to your capital cards.

Master your timing: We strongly recommend entering precise entry and exit dates and times. This information drives your future performance analysis and allows the app to build a complete profile of your trading style.

Market context: Use the "Before News" or "After News" buttons to classify your trades. This distinction is essential for understanding how you react to market volatility and adjusting your strategy accordingly.

[img:assets/help_center/add_trade_custom_actif.gif]

Manual asset addition: If the pair you trade does not appear in our list, click the gear icon located right next to the "Quantity" field. You can then manually add any asset, instrument, or currency pair.

Lasting customization: Once added, this asset becomes available for your future trades, allowing you to build your own library of trading instruments as you progress.

[img:assets/help_center/add_trade_screenshot_csv_note.gif]

Enrich your journal: Screenshots, Analysis, and Notes
A good journal is not limited to financial results; it documents the entire decision-making process. Use this section to archive every detail of your trades.

Visual proof (Screenshot): Do not just note your entry point. Attach a screenshot of your chart directly. This lets you instantly visualize the setup and market configuration at the time of taking the position.

Universal CSV import: No more tedious manual entry. PAYCHEK is compatible with 9 major platforms:

- MT4, MT5, TradingView, Tradovate, cTrader, NinjaTrader, Quantower, ATAS, and Rithmic.

Import your data in seconds and instantly retrieve your full session history.

Analysis and context:

- Analysis field: Did you work on a detailed report on your "My Analysis" page? Export it as a PDF and attach it directly to your trade to keep a record of your reasoning.
- Note field: Use this free space to note your feelings, doubts, or any particular remark that does not appear in your indicators. This is where you build your own experience trade by trade.

The "Black Box" of Your Trading: Performance and Discipline
The heart of PAYCHEK lies in its ability to transform your daily actions into actionable data for your Performance page. Each trade is analyzed not for its financial result, but for its compliance with your game plan.

1. Trade Typology: Principle vs Feeling
When adding a trade, define the nature of your execution:

- Principle: The trade was taken while strictly respecting your strategy, routine, and psychology.
- Feeling: The trade was taken under the influence of emotion.

[img:assets/help_center/add_trade_discipline_settings.png]

Customization (The Gear): Click the gear icon next to this section to adjust your preferences. By default, "Feeling" mode hides the analysis sections to simplify entry, but you can enable or disable them at will.

Daily session: Set a trade threshold after which your state automatically switches to "Feeling" to avoid overengagement.

2. Measuring Strategy Compliance (The Slider)
Use the percentage slider to quantify your level of strategy compliance on each trade. This figure is sent directly to your Performance page to correlate your rigor with your profitability.

[img:assets/help_center/add_trade_discipline_sections.png]

3. Non-Compliance Analysis: Checklist, Plan, and Mental State
For each trade, PAYCHEK lets you identify areas of failure:

- Strategy & Analysis: If you missed an element (e.g., support/resistance, technical signal), check it in the dynamic lists generated by your Strategy and My Analysis pages.
- Checklist: If your daily checklist was not respected, you will be notified before validating the trade entry.
- Mental State: View your current emotional state (e.g., Fear 46%). Check the influential factors so PAYCHEK can precisely calculate how your psychology impacts your Winrate.

4. Emotional Tags
Finish your entry by tagging the trade with the dominant emotion: FOMO, Tilt, Revenge, etc. This tag will, over time, let you visualize which types of emotions most quickly erode your capital.
